The Piece of Cake game is a fun, challenging puzzle where players stack cakes to match colorful patterns and complete levels.

## Option 1 — Browser / Web App

### Step 1 — Access the Game
1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Go to the official Piece of Cake game website.

### Step 2 — Create an Account (Optional)
1. Click on the "Sign Up" button if you want to save your progress.
2. Fill in the required information and verify your email address.

### Step 3 — Start Playing
1. Click on the "Play Now" button on the homepage.
2. Follow the on-screen instructions to begin your first level.

---

## Option 2 — Desktop App

### Step 1 — Download the App
1. Visit the official Piece of Cake game website.
2. Navigate to the "Download" section and choose the desktop version for your operating system.

### Step 2 — Install the App
1. Locate the downloaded file in your downloads folder.
2. Double-click the file and follow the installation prompts to set up the app on your computer.

    Ensure your device meets the minimum system requirements: a modern web browser or Windows/Mac operating system, and a stable internet connection for the web app.
